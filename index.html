Delete Your SpendWise Account

If you wish to delete your SpendWise account and associated data, you can request account deletion by contacting us.

Steps to Request Account Deletion

  1. Email us at smitpatel3433@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject: SpendWise Account Deletion Request
  3. Include the email address used to create your SpendWise account.

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Your account information (email address)
  • Your transaction history
  • Your budget settings

Data Retention

All requested data will be deleted within 7 days of receiving your request.
